About Us

  • Dr. G.P.Subash M.E., PhD.

    Founder & CEO

Dr.G. P. Subash is an Engineer, Researcher, Academician and Entrepreneur. A firm believer in “The best way to predict future is to create it”. Born in a small hamlet of Ponnai in Vellore District, Tamilnadu, educated in a Government school and Mechanical Engineering graduate from University of Madras. He obtained his Ph.D. from India’s, one of the most prestigious institute, Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT Delhi).

He has over 20 years of experience and his thrust areas of research are utilization of low- carbon emission alternative fuels such as Hydrogen, Compressed Natural Gas (CNG), Biodiesel, Ethanol, and Bio-CNG to replace fossil fuels. Prior to his current role, he was with Mahindra Waste to energy Solutions Ltd and Mahindra Research Valley.

He also worked as Senior Scientist with Industrial Research & Development, IIT Delhi on Research & Development projects related to alternate fuels. He has made technological collaboration with best industries in countries like Germany, Denmark and Sweden, for research and technology transfer. He has been invited as a speaker in the various national, international conferences, workshops and seminars.

He is a member of various professional bodies and his research achievements have been recognized and honoured with awards and medals.

Our Projects

Waste to Energy

Biogas solutions from Asbri started with the vision to provide a cost effective and economically viable solution for the organic waste issues. Our state of art technology, not only converts “Waste to Energy” but also helps to create “Wealth from Waste”.

Bio-CNG Technology

Asbri provides complete solution to upgrade the biogas into bio-CNG (CBG) as per BIS-16087 standards.

Replacing Commercial LPG

Bio-CNG Calorific value is comparable with LPG and the combustion characteristics are better than LPG. Bio-CNG is safer than LPG and reduces carbon footprint by 87% compared to fossil fuels. Thus, it is an Enviro and Eco- friendly fuel.
